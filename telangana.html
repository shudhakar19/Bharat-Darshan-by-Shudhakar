Telangana

"The Gateway to the South"

Quick Facts

Capital: Hyderabad
Chief Minister: Revanth Reddy
Governor: Tamilisai Soundararajan
Official Language: Telugu
Population: ~3.8 crore
Area: 112,077 sq km
Literacy Rate: 72.8%
State Animal: Spotted Deer
State Bird: Indian Roller
State Flower: Tanner's Cassia

Popular Tourist Attractions

Charminar

Hyderabad’s iconic monument known for its Indo-Islamic architecture.

Ramoji Film City

One of the world’s largest film studios and a major tourist attraction.

Warangal Fort

Historic ruins representing Kakatiya dynasty's architectural excellence.

Nature & Wildlife

Home to Nagarjunasagar-Srisailam Tiger Reserve, Pocharam Wildlife Sanctuary, and forested hill regions rich in biodiversity.

Famous Personalities

  • PV Narasimha Rao: Former Prime Minister of India.
  • K. Chandrashekar Rao: First Chief Minister of Telangana.
  • Sania Mirza: Indian tennis icon born in Hyderabad.

Local Handicrafts

Renowned for Pochampally Ikat saris, Bidriware metal crafts, Nirmal paintings, and silver filigree work.

Climate

Hot and dry summers, mild winters, and seasonal rainfall from the southwest monsoon.

Culture & Festivals

Dance

Perini Shivatandavam and Lambadi dances represent the folk spirit of Telangana.

Festivals

Bathukamma, Bonalu, and Sankranti are celebrated with regional pride and floral decorations.

Food

Famous for spicy Hyderabadi Biryani, haleem, and traditional Telangana cuisine.

Attire

Women wear colorful saris, while men wear dhotis and turbans, often with a Telangana scarf.

Did You Know? Telangana was formed on 2nd June 2014, becoming the 29th state of India, and Hyderabad continues to serve as joint capital with Andhra Pradesh for a limited period.
