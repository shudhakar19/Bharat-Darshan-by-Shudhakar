Andhra Pradesh

"The Kohinoor of India"

Quick Facts

Capital: Amaravati
Chief Minister: Y. S. Jagan Mohan Reddy
Governor: S. Abdul Nazeer
Official Language: Telugu
Population: ~5 crore
Area: 162,975 sq km
Literacy Rate: 67.4%
State Animal: Blackbuck
State Bird: Indian Roller
State Flower: Jasmine

Popular Tourist Attractions

Tirupati

One of the richest and most visited pilgrimage sites in the world.

Araku Valley

Scenic hill station with coffee plantations and tribal culture.

Visakhapatnam

Beautiful coastal city with beaches, hills, and Buddhist heritage sites.

Nature & Wildlife

Andhra Pradesh is home to lush forests, estuaries, and diverse wildlife. Popular spots include the Nagarjunasagar-Srisailam Tiger Reserve, Papikonda National Park, and Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary known for mangroves and estuarine crocodiles.

Famous Personalities

  • N. T. Rama Rao: Legendary actor and former Chief Minister.
  • Satya Nadella: CEO of Microsoft, born in Hyderabad (then in AP).
  • P. V. Narasimha Rao: Former Prime Minister of India.
  • C. Narayana Reddy: Renowned Telugu poet and Jnanpith Awardee.

Climate

Andhra Pradesh has a tropical climate with hot summers, moderate winters, and heavy rainfall during the monsoon. Coastal regions remain humid throughout the year.

Culture & Festivals

Dance & Music

Home of the classical dance form Kuchipudi and vibrant Carnatic music.

Festivals

Ugadi, Sankranti, and Dasara are widely celebrated with grandeur and traditions.

Cuisine

Famous for spicy dishes like Andhra biryani, gongura pickle, and pulihora.

Clothing

Men wear dhoti and shirt while women wear sarees with traditional jewelry.

Did You Know? Andhra Pradesh has the second longest coastline in India and is a major exporter of seafood.
