Delhi

"The Heart of India"

Quick Facts

Capital: Delhi
Chief Minister: Arvind Kejriwal
Lieutenant Governor: V. K. Saxena
Official Language: Hindi
Population: ~3.2 crore
Area: 1,484 sq km
Literacy Rate: 88.7%
State Animal: Nilgai
State Bird: House Sparrow
State Flower: Alfalfa

Popular Tourist Attractions

India Gate

Iconic war memorial and evening hotspot.

Qutub Minar

UNESCO world heritage site built in 1192.

Red Fort

Symbol of Mughal grandeur and Independence Day celebrations.

Nature & Wildlife

Delhi Ridge and Asola Bhatti Wildlife Sanctuary offer green escapes with species like nilgai, porcupine, and civets.

Famous Personalities

  • Bhagat Singh: Celebrated freedom fighter who operated from Delhi during key actions.
  • Lata Mangeshkar: Frequent performer at Delhi events; her voice echoes in national pride.
  • Amitabh Bachchan: Attended school in Delhi before rising to stardom.

Local Handicrafts

Home to Zardozi embroidery, pottery from Khurja, brassware, and Meenakari work found in old Delhi markets.

Culture & Festivals

Festivals

Celebrates Diwali, Holi, Eid, Christmas, and Republic Day parade at Rajpath.

Food

Famous for street food like chole bhature, parathas, kebabs, and golgappas.

Dance & Music

Blend of classical performances like Kathak and modern music events across the capital.

Did You Know? Delhi is one of the most ancient cities in the world with habitation dating back to the 6th century BCE.
