Andaman and Nicobar Islands

"Where Nature Meets Serenity"

Quick Facts

Capital: Port Blair
Administrator: Admiral D K Joshi (Retd.)
Official Languages: Hindi, English
Population: ~4 lakh
Area: 8,249 sq km
Literacy Rate: 86.63%
State Animal: Dugong
State Bird: Andaman Wood Pigeon
State Flower: Andaman Pyinma

Popular Tourist Attractions

Radhanagar Beach

Rated among Asia’s best beaches on Havelock Island.

Cellular Jail

A colonial prison that tells stories of Indian freedom fighters.

Baratang Island

Famous for limestone caves and mud volcanoes.

Nature & Wildlife

The islands boast coral reefs, mangroves, tropical rainforests, and marine biodiversity including rare sea creatures like Dugongs and turtles.

Famous Personalities

  • Veer Savarkar: Imprisoned in Cellular Jail during India's freedom struggle.

Local Handicrafts

Shell crafts, wood carvings, and cane goods reflect the islanders’ rich artisan skills.

Climate

The islands have a tropical climate with warm temperatures year-round and heavy monsoon rains from May to September.

Culture & Festivals

Tribes

Home to indigenous tribes like Jarawas, Sentinelese, and Nicobarese.

Food

Seafood, coconut-based dishes, and tropical fruits are prominent.

Festivals

Island Tourism Festival and Subhash Mela are celebrated with enthusiasm.

Did You Know? The Barren Island in Andaman is the only active volcano in India!
