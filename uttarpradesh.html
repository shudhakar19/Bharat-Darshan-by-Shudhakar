Uttar Pradesh

"The Heartland of India"

Quick Facts

Capital: Lucknow
Chief Minister: Yogi Adityanath
Governor: Anandiben Patel
Official Languages: Hindi, Urdu
Population: ~24 crore
Area: 243,286 sq km
Literacy Rate: 67.68%
State Animal: Swamp Deer
State Bird: Sarus Crane
State Flower: Palash (Butea monosperma)

Popular Tourist Attractions

Taj Mahal

One of the Seven Wonders of the World located in Agra.

Varanasi

Ancient city on the banks of Ganga, spiritual capital of India.

Ayodhya

Birthplace of Lord Ram and home to the majestic Ram Mandir.

Nature & Wildlife

Uttar Pradesh hosts Dudhwa National Park and Katarniaghat Wildlife Sanctuary, home to tigers, swamp deer, elephants, and migratory birds.

Famous Personalities

  • Atal Bihari Vajpayee: Former Prime Minister of India.
  • Munshi Premchand: Iconic Hindi-Urdu writer.
  • Lata Mangeshkar: Legendary playback singer (roots in Varanasi).

Local Handicrafts

Chikankari embroidery, zardozi work, Banarasi silk sarees, and brassware from Moradabad highlight the artistic excellence of the region.

Climate

Uttar Pradesh experiences hot summers, a monsoon season from June to September, and cool winters, especially in the northern plains.

Culture & Festivals

Dance

Kathak, one of the eight classical dances of India, originated here.

Festivals

Diwali in Ayodhya, Holi in Mathura, and Kumbh Mela in Prayagraj are celebrated grandly.

Food

Famous for Awadhi biryani, tikkas, kebabs, and sweets like peda and jalebi.
