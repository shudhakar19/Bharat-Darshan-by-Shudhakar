Bihar

"Land of Enlightenment"

Quick Facts

Capital: Patna
Chief Minister: Nitish Kumar
Governor: Rajendra Arlekar
Official Languages: Hindi, Urdu
Population: ~12 crore
Area: 94,163 sq km
Literacy Rate: 63.82%
State Animal: Gaur (Indian Bison)
State Bird: House Sparrow
State Flower: Kachnar

Popular Tourist Attractions

Bodh Gaya

Where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi tree.

Nalanda University

One of the world’s oldest universities and a center of learning.

Rajgir

Ancient city with hot springs and Buddhist, Jain heritage.

Nature & Wildlife

Bihar hosts Valmiki National Park, home to tigers, elephants, and rich flora. The Ganges plains nurture diverse bird species too.

Famous Personalities

  • Dr. Rajendra Prasad: India’s first President.
  • Jayaprakash Narayan: Freedom fighter and social reformer.
  • Kajol: Celebrated Bollywood actress born in Bihar.

Local Handicrafts

Madhubani paintings, Sikki grass crafts, and Sujni embroidery reflect Bihar’s artistic legacy.

Climate

Bihar has a humid subtropical climate with hot summers, monsoon rains, and mild winters.

Culture & Festivals

Festivals

Chhath Puja is celebrated with devotion to the Sun God along riverbanks.

Dance & Music

Bhojpuri folk songs, Jat-Jatin and Bidesia dances are native to Bihar.

Cuisine

Litti Chokha, Khaja, and Thekua are traditional favorites.

Clothing

Sarees with Madhubani prints for women, dhoti-kurta for men.

Did You Know? Nalanda was one of the first residential universities in the world, with over 10,000 students.
