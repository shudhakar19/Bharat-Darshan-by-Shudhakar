Odisha

"The Soul of Incredible India"

Quick Facts

Capital: Bhubaneswar
Chief Minister: Naveen Patnaik
Governor: Raghubar Das
Official Language: Odia
Population: ~4.5 crore
Area: 155,707 sq km
Literacy Rate: 72.87%
State Animal: Sambar Deer
State Bird: Indian Roller
State Flower: Ashoka Flower

Popular Tourist Attractions

Konark Sun Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its architectural brilliance.

Puri Jagannath Temple

A sacred pilgrimage site and home to the grand Rath Yatra festival.

Chilika Lake

Asia’s largest brackish water lagoon, a paradise for bird watchers.

Nature & Wildlife

Odisha has rich biodiversity with reserves like Simlipal, Bhitarkanika, and Satkosia for elephants, crocodiles, and tigers.

Famous Personalities

  • Naveen Patnaik: One of the longest-serving CMs of India.
  • Sudarsan Pattnaik: Internationally acclaimed sand artist.
  • Bijay Mohanty: Iconic Odia film actor.

Local Handicrafts

Odisha is famous for Pattachitra paintings, silver filigree work from Cuttack, and handwoven Sambalpuri sarees.

Climate

Odisha has a tropical climate with hot summers, heavy monsoons, and mild winters.

Culture & Festivals

Dance

Odissi, one of the oldest classical dance forms, originated in Odisha.

Festivals

Rath Yatra is the most celebrated festival in Puri with huge chariots.

Food

Staples include rice, dalma, and sweets like Rasgulla and Chhena Poda.

Attire

Sambalpuri handwoven sarees and dhotis reflect the state’s textile heritage.

Did You Know? The Rasgulla was originally invented in Odisha before spreading to Bengal!
